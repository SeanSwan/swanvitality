About Us

Swan Vitality Personal Trainers is a group of Elite trainers who work together to build a stronger community though health and fitness. Established July 2000 by Trainer and CEO Sean Swan. Since 2000 we have seen the trends of processed foods rise as well as cancer in America. Were here to give people the knowledge and understanding of the poisons that your body is ingesting daily and give you the tools to reverse the damage we have so unknowingly been inflicting to ourselves for so many years whether it be posture related or chemical. From child to senior. Professional Athlete to soccer mom we have a customized plan made specifically for your needs and goals. We train locally in the Anaheim Hills Area doing private training and exercise classes at your Home, Park or our Private Gym N.A.S.M / N.C.E.P. / N.A.F.S.H / A.S.F.A Certified Literally thousands of hours hands on training and results!
